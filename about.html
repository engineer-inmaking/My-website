About

Gandharva is the official drama club of SGGSIE&T. Team gandharva has been providing perfect platform for dramatically inclined people in the institutes. As a club, we perform at various college events, participate in notable competitions while also arranging our own. The Gandharva curriculum includes regular practice meets & workshop sessions, with occasional outings and trips. Currently a Club of 24 members which includes Writers, Directors, Actors, Narrators, Poets,Visual artists, media handlers & reel makers.We are eagerly waiting to welcome and include more dramatical enthusiasts in the Gandharva family.
